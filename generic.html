I have participated in 2 hackathons - Insurtech Data Hackathon organized by FWD Malaysia and 1337 Ventures and PayHack 2023 organized by JomHack and PayNet.

In the first hackathon, Insurtech Data Hackathon, our team managed to get second place. The idea was to use social media data and perform sentiment analysis to determine whether the person is applicable for certain insurance. In this case, we scraped tweets from Twitter and use BERT analysis to determine the eligibility of that user. After all, in this world where constantly sharing and using social media has become a norm, one could say how we behave in social media reflects to our behaviour in the real world. This idea grabbed us second place!

In the second hackathon, PayHack 2023, our team managed to get third place. The idea was to utilize the latest hype in technology - Large Language Model. For a use case example, everyone who uses apps like Grab Food or Shopee knows the struggle when it comes to requesting for a refund. Our idea allows the user to simply make a simple command like "Please help me to refund my previous order" and with the use of OpenAI ChatGPT, it will process the requests and automatically perform the necessary steps to submit the request. This idea grabbed us third place!
